Our Services

Offering Guidance and Support

To provide guidance and support to clients as the navigate through challenging life situations to develop effective coping mechanisms, improve problem-solving skills and enhance self esteem. To help clients better understand their mental health concerns and develop strategies for self-care.

Maintaining Ethical Standards

To maintain confidentiality, respect, clients, autonomy and rights, and uphold professional boundaries. To provide professional supervision or consultation to ensure the highest quality of care.

Conducting Assessments

To gather, information through interviews, standardised tests and other assessment tools to evaluate client’s concerns, strengths and needs that provides in formulating appropriate treatment plans and interventions.

Provide Therapy and Counselling

To provide therapy and counselling, services to individuals, couples, families or groups. To create a safe and non-judgemental environment where clients can discuss their concerns, emotions and experiences to utilise various therapeutic approaches such as cognitive behavioural therapy, psychodynamic therapy, humanistic therapy to address client specific needs.

Developing Treatment Plans

To develop personalised treatment plans based on assessment and client collaboration. These plans outline the therapeutic goals, strategies and interventions. Treatment plans are tailored to the client's unique circumstances and involve setting specific objectives, identified targeted areas for growth and change, and establishing a timeline for therapy.

Counselling Services

To address the concerns such as anxiety, depression, psychosis, anger management, post, traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), adjustment, behavioural/mood, changes, eating concerns, self-esteem, body, image, grief and loss, relationship, conflicts,suicidality etc.
